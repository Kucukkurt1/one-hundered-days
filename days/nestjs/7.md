## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **`repository.save()`:** A TypeORM method that can be used to save a new entity or update an existing one.
*   **`repository.find()`:** A TypeORM method to retrieve multiple records from a table.
*   **`repository.findOne()`:** A TypeORM method to retrieve a single record from a table.
*   **`repository.update()`:** A TypeORM method to update records in a table.
*   **`repository.delete()`:** A TypeORM method to delete records from a table.
